About

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Stanford University, where I lead the Responsible AI Research Lab. My work focuses on developing ethical artificial intelligence systems that are transparent, fair, and beneficial to society.

Before joining Stanford, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at MIT's Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL). I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University in 2015.

Research Interests

Explainable AI

Developing techniques to make AI decision-making processes more transparent and understandable.

AI Fairness

Investigating algorithms to detect and mitigate biases in AI systems.

Context-Aware NLP

Creating language models that better understand contextual nuances.
